# Tramacere et al. (2010)

### Item-by-item Scoring Justification:

**Review question clearly stated (2/2):** Clear objective to assess dose-risk relation between alcohol and pancreatic cancer.

**Inclusion criteria appropriate (2/2):** Selected studies with ≥3 exposure categories and estimable RRs/ORs.

**Search strategy appropriate (2/2):** Systematic PubMed search with MOOSE adherence and backward citation.

**Sources/resources adequate (2/2):** Used multiple databases and contacted authors for unpublished data.

**Appraisal criteria appropriate (2/2):** Custom structured schema assessing 10 quality dimensions per study (Table 2, p. 5).

**Independent critical appraisal (2/2):** Two authors independently reviewed and scored studies.

**Minimized extraction errors (2/2):** Detailed extraction procedures, including verification of confounder adjustment and case distributions.

**Combination of studies appropriate (2/2):** Random-effects, stratified analyses, and non-linear dose-response meta-regression models.

**Publication bias assessed (1/2):** No mention of Egger’s/Begg’s test or funnel plot; indirect bias explored via subgroup analysis.

**Recommendations supported by data (2/2):** Strong conclusions based on clear dose-risk relationships.

**Directives for new research appropriate (2/2):** Called for data by beverage type, higher consumption levels, and better confounder adjustment.
