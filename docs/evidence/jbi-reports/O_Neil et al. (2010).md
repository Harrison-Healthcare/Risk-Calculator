# O_Neil et al. (2010)

### Item-by-item Scoring Justification:

**Criteria for inclusion clearly defined (2/2):** Clear inclusion/exclusion criteria, including age range and removal of pregnant/lactating women.

**Study subjects and setting described in detail (2/2):** Detailed demographics and setting description (nationally representative NHANES data).

**Exposure measured validly and reliably (2/2):** Whole-grain intake quantified using the USDA MPED, a robust and standardized database.

**Objective, standard criteria used for condition measurement (2/2):** HEI used for diet quality, with validated methods.

**Confounding factors identified (2/2):** Covariates including energy intake, age, sex, and ethnicity considered.

**Strategies to deal with confounding stated (2/2):** Confounding addressed via adjusted regression models.

**Outcomes measured validly and reliably (2/2):** Standardized NHANES dietary methods and USDA nutrient databases.

**Appropriate statistical analysis used (2/2):** Used SUDAAN with sample weights, linear trend tests, and proper multivariate modeling.
